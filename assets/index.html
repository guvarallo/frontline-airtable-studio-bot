Twilio Serverless Frontline Integration Service

Congratulations you just created a new Twilio Serverless Integration Service for Twilio Frontline.

What is this?

This service provides a back-end for your Twilio Frontline Service that connects your users to an Airtable-based CRM.

All functions exposed by this service are protected by Twilio Webhook authorization tokens, so you won't be able to make requests to them from another app like postman or curl.
